About

Ana Berrocal is a Product Designer and Creative based in Brooklyn, NY. With a background in frontend development and photography, she brings both technical precision and a strong visual eye to every project.

She has spent the last several years shipping digital products for tens of thousands of users, leading design from concept through code. Currently at United Airlines, she owns end-to-end design for one of the company's most critical platforms. She is always looking for the next thing to make better.
