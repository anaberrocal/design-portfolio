About

“I design, I build, I photograph. Everything I make is grounded in craft and curiosity.”

Senior Product Designer based in Brooklyn. I have a background in frontend development and formal photography training at The Art Institute of Fort Lauderdale. I'm currently at United Airlines, where I have designed and shipped products for 15,000+ users, built component libraries from scratch, and performed in person user testing. My design work is driven by a desire to create meaningful, human-centered experiences with a high level of polish and attention to detail. My photography experience informs my design work and allows me to bring a unique perspective to visual storytelling. I'm always eager to take on new challenges and collaborate with others who share my passion for craft and creativity.
